Rate Design {{ Spiel_Counter }} - Periodic Tariff

 
In the Periodic Tariff rate design, you can place a bid for renewable energy between 1.0 and 5.0 monetary units in steps of 0.5. Renewable energy is then sold to you at your bid if available over both days that you'll play. All non-renewable generation costs 5.0. You can only place one bid for both days.

Your competition is also placing a bid. You will then be ranked based on those competing bids. This ranking will be communicated to you. Whoever bids more, is supplied with renewable energy first and demand is covered by renewable energy until no more renewable supply is available.
 
In other words, if you bid lower prices, you might receive renewable generation for a cheaper price, but with a lower bid you risk to be ranked second and if not enough renewable energy is available, you have to pay for non-renewable generation.
 
You are able to move your demand between morning and afternoon within a day. This could help you to get assigned more renewable generation if you move your demand to a period with more available renewable supply. Remember that shifting demand leads to a cost of 1 monetary unit per shifted unit.
 
Characterization of PET:
Price change frequency: Constant prices
Price determination: Based on bids
Price bids by consumer: Once
 
You will now be able to play a trial run. The first day will start directly after the trial run and you can place a new bid after the trial run. Please click Continue when you’re ready.
